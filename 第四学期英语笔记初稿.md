**📙 第四学期英语笔记：短语、句型与语法（含例句）**

**1. 动词搭配与句型**

| 短语/句型 | 词性/说明 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| raise money for | 动词短语 | 为……筹款 | *The students **raised money for** the local animal shelter.* |
| raise | v. | 举起；养育；筹集 | *He **raised** his hand to ask a question.* |
| volunteer to do sth | 动词短语 | 自愿做某事 | *She **volunteered to help** clean up the park.* |
| offer to do sth | 动词短语 | 主动提出做某事 | *He **offered to carry** the heavy box for me.* |
| offer sb sth / offer sth to sb | 动词短语 | 给某人提供某物 | *She **offered me a cup of tea**. / She **offered a cup of tea to me**.* |
| belong to | 动词短语 (无被动) | 属于 | *This book **belongs to** my brother.* |
| have difficulty in doing sth | 句型 | 做某事有困难 | *I **have difficulty (in) understanding** his accent.* |
| make sb do sth (被动) | 语法要点 | 使役动词被动还原to | *I **was made to rewrite** the homework by the teacher.* |
| get rid of | 动词短语 | 除去；摆脱 | *We need to **get rid of** these old newspapers.* |
| make sb feel welcome | 动词短语 | 让某人感到受欢迎 | *The host tried to **make everyone feel welcome**.* |
| go to sb for help | 动词短语 | 向某人求助 | *If you’re lost, you can **go to a policeman for help**.* |
| give ... a good impression / make a good impression on | 动词短语 | 给……留下好印象 | *He **gave** me **a very good impression**. / She **made a good impression on** the interviewer.* |
| impress sth on sb | 动词短语 | 使某人意识到某事 | *The teacher tried to **impress** the importance of safety **on** the children.* |
| hold a meeting | 动词短语 | 举行会议 | *The board will **hold a meeting** next Monday.* |
| hold sth up | 动词短语 | 举起；耽搁 | *He **held up** the trophy in victory. / The traffic accident **held us up** for hours.* |
| remind sb that | 动词短语 | 提醒某人…… | *Please **remind me that** I have a meeting at 3.* |
| remind sb about sth | 动词短语 | 提醒某人关于某事 | *He **reminded me about** the party this weekend.* |
| remind sb of sth | 动词短语 | 使某人想起某事 | *This song **reminds me of** my childhood.* |
| remind sb to do sth | 动词短语 | 提醒某人做某事 | *Mom **reminded me to take** my medicine.* |
| behave oneself | 动词短语 | 举止得体；守规矩 | *Please **behave yourself** at the dinner party.* |
| listen to | 动词短语 | 听…… | *I like to **listen to** music before sleeping.* |
| why don’t / why not + 原形 | 句型 | 为什么不……呢？ | ***Why don’t you ask** for help? / **Why not ask** for help?* |
| you’d better do ... | 句型 (you had better) | 你最好做…… | ***You’d better take** an umbrella. It looks like rain.* |
| go on to do sth | 动词短语 | 接着做另一件新的事 | *He finished his homework and **went on to play** games.* |
| go on doing sth | 动词短语 | 继续做之前没做完的事 | *He **went on reading** until midnight.* |
| hear from | 动词短语 | 收到某人的来信 | *I haven’t **heard from** him for a long time.* |
| look into one’s eyes | 动词短语 | 直视某人的眼睛 | *He **looked into her eyes** and smiled.* |
| avoid doing sth | 动词短语 | 避免做某事 | *You should **avoid driving** during rush hour.* |
| combine A with B | 动词短语 | 将A与B结合 | *We should **combine** theory **with** practice.* |
| be influenced by | 动词短语 | 被……影响 | *His writing is greatly **influenced by** Hemingway.* |
| bring ... into ... | 动词短语 | 把……引进……；带入 | *She **brought** her experience **into** the new project.* |
| leave for | 动词短语 | 动身去 | *He **left for** New York this morning.* |
| make advances in sth | 动词短语 | 在……方面取得进步 | *Scientists have **made great advances in** space exploration.* |
| be prepared to do sth | 形容词短语 | 准备好做某事 | *I **am prepared to face** any challenge.* |
| be split into | 动词短语 (被动) | 被分成…… | *The class **was split into** three groups.* |
| separate A from B | 动词短语 | 把A和B分开 | *A wall **separates** the garden **from** the main road.* |
| separate ... into ... | 动词短语 | 把……分成…… | *The teacher **separated** the students **into** teams.* |
| graduate from | 动词短语 | 从……毕业 | *She **graduated from** college last year.* |
| make up one’s mind | 动词短语 | 下定决心 | *I’ve **made up my mind to study** abroad.* |
| dedicate/devote one’s mind to doing | 动词短语 | 全身心投入做某事 | *She **dedicated her mind to solving** the puzzle.* |
| work with... | 动词短语 | 处理……；与……共事 | *I enjoy **working with** children.* |
| keep sth alive | 动词短语 | 使某物保持活力/生机 | *Traditions help **keep our culture alive**.* |
| be shaped into | 动词短语 (被动) | 被塑造成…… | *The soft clay can easily **be shaped into** a bowl.* |

**2. "be" 动词与介词短语**

| 短语 | 词性/说明 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| be damaged by | 形容词短语 | 被……损坏 | *The house **was badly damaged by** the fire.* |
| be separated into | 形容词短语 | 被分成…… | *The course **is separated into** several modules.* |
| be required to do sth | 句型 | 被要求做某事 | *All visitors **are required to show** their ID.* |
| be used to do sth | 句型 | 被用来做某事 | *This tool **is used to cut** glass.* |
| pay attention to | 动词短语 | 注意；留意 | *Please **pay attention to** what the teacher is saying.* |

**3. 其他常用短语与表达**

| 类别 | 短语 | 词性/说明 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **时间与顺序** | right away / at once | adv. | 立刻，马上 | *I’ll call you back **right away**.* |
| | for the first time | adv. 短语 | 第一次 | *She saw the ocean **for the first time**.* |
| **逻辑与连接** | in many cases | adv. 短语 | 在很多情况下 | ***In many cases**, patience is the key.* |
| | in any case | adv. 短语 | 无论如何 | ***In any case**, we should be prepared.* |
| | it is no surprise that | 句型 | 毫不奇怪…… | ***It is no surprise that** he won the award.* |
| | such (a/an) adj n. that ... | 句型 | 如此……以至于…… | *It was **such a good movie that** I watched it twice.* |
| **状态与感受** | in a good/bad mood | 介词短语 | 心情好/坏 | *Leave him alone. He’s **in a bad mood**.* |
| | in the mood for sth | 介词短语 | 有心情做某事 | *I’m not really **in the mood for** a party.* |
| | in no mood for sth | 介词短语 | 没心情做某事 | *He was **in no mood for** jokes.* |
| **介词搭配辨析** | belong to / at / on | 介词搭配辨析 | **belong to**: 属于（某人/某物）；<br>**belong at**: 适合待在（某处）；<br>**belong on**: 应放在（某处） | *This book **belongs to** me. / She feels she doesn‘t **belong at** this party. / These files **belong on** the top shelf.* |
| **其它名词性短语** | the shape of ... | n. | ……的形状 | *They discussed **the shape of** the new logo.* |
| | wall painting | n. | 壁画 | *The cave is famous for its ancient **wall paintings**.* |

**4. 语法与词汇要点**

| 项目 | 词性/说明 | 中文释义 | 例句/备注 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **接动名词的动词 (V-ing)** | enjoy, finish, mind, suggest, consider, deny, keep, practice, miss, avoid, imagine, admit 等 | 这些动词后只能接`doing` | *She **enjoys reading**. / He **denies stealing** the money. / I **miss seeing** you.* |
| **old vs. elder** | adj. | **old**: 用法广；**elder**: 仅用于人 | *He is my **elder** brother. / This is an **old** book.* |
| **ask for permission** | 固定搭配 | 请求许可 | *You must **ask for permission** to leave early.* |
| **in one’s spare time** | 介词短语 | 在某人的空闲时间 | *She reads a lot **in her spare time**.* |
| **expression** | n. [C/U] | 表达；表情 | *He had a confused **expression** on his face.* |
| **scene** | n. [C usually sing.] | 现场；景象 | *The police arrived at the **scene** of the crime.* |
| **behave at table** | 动词短语/固定搭配 | 餐桌礼仪 | *Children should learn to **behave at table**.* |
| **smile at** | 动词短语 | 对……微笑 | *She **smiled at** the baby.* |
| **look down** | 动词短语 | 向下看 | *He **looked down** from the top of the tower.* |
| **is cut in** | 语法/被动 | 被切；被插话 | *Water **is cut in** during the drought.* (也可指插嘴，如 *He **was cut in** while speaking.*) |
| **come with** | 动词短语 | 与……一起；附带 | *The phone **comes with** a charger.* |
| **breakthrough** | n. | 突破 | *Scientists made a major **breakthrough** in cancer research.* |
| **with the help of / with one’s help** | 介词短语 | 在……的帮助下 | ***With the help of** a tutor, he passed the test.* |
| **advance** | v./n. | 前进；进步 | *The army **advanced** slowly. / We’ve made an **advance**.* |
| **among (the) 最高级** | prep. | 在……之中 (用于范围) | *He is **among the best** students in the class.* |
| **begin working on** | 动词短语 | 开始从事 | *They **began working on** the project last week.* |
| **breathtaking** | adj. | 令人惊叹的 | *The view from the mountain is **breathtaking**.* |
| **work as** | 动词短语 | 担任……工作 | *She **works as** a software engineer.* |
| **limit the number of** | 动词短语 | 限制……的数量 | *They decided to **limit the number of** attendees.* |
| **the shape of** | 名词短语 | ……的形状 | *The artist painted **the shape of** a hand.* |
